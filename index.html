About Our Mission

To extract value from Big Data by harnessing cutting-edge statistical methods and machine learning algorithms, and to deepen our understanding of Big Data with the goal of providing actionable intelligence to society.
•
The scope of our interest includes, but is not limited to, Bayesian methodology and computation, Bayesian nonparametrics, scalable Bayesian inference, deep learning algorithms, and missing data problems.
•
The Data Science Lab at Yonsei University is looking for fully funded PhD students and post-doctoral researchers.
•
Please contact directly Professor Park via email with your resume.
